Why Bartact Leads on the Gladiator JT

The Gladiator JT is a unique platform — it shares the Wrangler's interior layout but extends it with a longer cab and the truck bed. That means most generic seat covers either fit wrong or look sloppy. Bartact builds specifically for the JT, which means the install is clean, the coverage is complete, and the MOLLE panels actually align with how the interior is designed to be used.

Beyond seat covers, Bartact's grab handles and door bags are the two accessories that Gladiator owners mention most in real-world reviews. The grab handles because the JT's doors-off capability makes them genuinely necessary. The door bags because the cabin storage equation on a Gladiator is different from a two-door Wrangler, and those door panels have real dead space that Bartact specifically addressed.
